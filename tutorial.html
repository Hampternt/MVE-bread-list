Workflow
1
Select your route from the dropdown
2
Sorting Stage — tick each bread type as you sort it into the van Collapse it when done to get it out of the way
3
Tap any card to mark an item as packed Packed items sink to the bottom of their customer group
4
Hold a card for half a second to flag it as missing Phone will vibrate to confirm. Hold again to undo.
5
On a missing card — tap "Note replacement" to log a substitute Only available for items that accept alternatives
